Stress is a major risk for chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease, metabolic diseases, kidney diseases, cancer and neurodegenerative diseases. The system aims at maintaining user records such as school scheduled activities and their emotions over a period of time and allow the user to find out how such activities correlated with their emotions at different times.
